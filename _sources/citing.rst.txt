Citing
======

You can cite KQCircuits in your projects and publications.
Version-specific DOIs and other information in many citation formats,
including ``.bib``, are found on Zenodo. Click the badge below to go to Zenodo.

.. image:: https://zenodo.org/badge/DOI/10.5281/zenodo.4944796.svg
   :target: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.4944796
   :alt: DOI

the following ``.bib`` file can be used::
